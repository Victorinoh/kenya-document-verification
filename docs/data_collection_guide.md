# Data Collection Guide

## Objective
Collect minimum 100 document images for training the AI model

## Priority Collection Order
1. Kenyan National ID (most common, most important)
2. KCSE Certificate (second priority)
3. Passport (third priority)

## How to Collect Samples

### Method 1: Your Own Documents (Easiest)
- Scan or photograph your own ID, certificates
- Anonymize before saving (blur sensitive info)
- Best quality - you control the process

### Method 2: Friends and Family
- Ask permission first
- Anonymize immediately
- Aim for variety (different years, types)

### Method 3: Online Public Sources
- KNEC sample certificates (official website)
- Government press releases with document samples
- News articles showing document samples

## Quality Requirements
- Resolution: Minimum 300 DPI
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Size: At least 1000x600 pixels
- Lighting: Even, no shadows or glare
- Angle: Straight on (not tilted)
- Focus: Sharp, all text readable

## How to Anonymize Documents
1. Open image in Paint or any editor
2. Draw black rectangles over:
   - ID Number
   - Full Name
   - Date of Birth
   - Photo
3. Save as new file

## Naming Convention
national_id_genuine_001.jpg
national_id_genuine_002.jpg
kcse_cert_genuine_001.jpg
